WELCOME TO THE NEIGHBOURHOOD WATCH OF DE LA HAYE, BELLVILLE.

OUR VISION: TO LIVE AND OPERATE IN A CRIME FREE NEIGHBOURHOOD.

 

To make the Area of De La Haye Neighbourhood Watch crime free by collaborating with the

S.A. Police Service, Cape Town City Council and other Stakeholders.

Our Aims are:

(a) To nurture a sense of unity, dedication and good neighbourly relations among the residents or business in the De la Haye area.

(b) To serve as a watchdog for the De la Haye community, local business and the local Police Services and thereby assisting the local Police Services to safeguard its community against criminal activities and to strive for a safe and secure environment.

(c) To provide a channel through which the De la Haye community can play a meaningful role in the preservation of a safe and secure environment.

As of 20/05/2021 the De La Haye Neighbourhood Watch was officially accredited by the Western Cape Department of Community Safety as a recognised Neighbourhood Watch.
